Research Interest

 I am a third-year M.S student in Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Hongbin Sun and Dr. Xinwei Shen. My research interest lies in the planning and operation of active distribution network and integrated energy system with energy storage systems (ESSs):
  • A two-stage stochastic programming for distribution systems considering ESS degradation is proposed where GMM is adopted to generate representative scenarios.
  • Energy arbitrage and regulation services provided by ESSs are co-optimized while determining ESSs' siting and sizing.
  • By computing a rational average of all decision variables and adopting gap-dependent penalty factors, a modified PH algorithm with parallel computing is introduced to improve the computation efficiency.
