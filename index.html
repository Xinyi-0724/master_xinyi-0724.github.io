Research Interest

 I am a third-year M.S student in Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Hongbin Sun and Dr. Xinwei Shen. My research interest lies in the planning and operation of active distribution network. Currently, I focus on the following topics:
  • Two-stage stochastic programming for distribution systems considering energy arbitrage and regulation services provided by ESSs;
  • N-1 criterion and reliability assessment in the multistage expansion planning model;
  • The modified PH algorithm with parallel computing to improve the computation efficiency of MILP models.
